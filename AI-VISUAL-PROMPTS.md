## Quick Generation Tips

1. **Gemini Imagen 3**: Best for photorealistic and illustration styles. Use "high quality" and "detailed" keywords.
2. **Veo 3.1**: For video loops. Request "seamless loop" and specify 4K/24fps. Keep under 10 seconds.
3. **Flux models**: Great for artistic/abstract visuals. Higher step counts (20-30) give more detail.
4. **Post-processing**:
   - Convert all images to `.webp` format for best web performance
   - Use TinyPNG/Squoosh to compress without quality loss
   - Target: Hero images <200KB, Card images <100KB, Thumbnails <50KB
5. **Consistency**: Always include the color hex codes in prompts to maintain brand consistency across all visuals.
